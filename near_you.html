Getting
to Know
Technology

Virtual orientation will involve a variety of technology. Here is an overview of what you will be using during Orientation and as an Arizona student, as well as the technical support that is available to you 24/7.

desktop_windows

Desire 2 Learn

D2L (Desire2Learn) provides instructors and students with an online space for traditional classroom courses, online classes, or hybrids.

support

UITS

The 24/7 Support Center technical consultants are available in person, by phone, or over chat to assist with campus applications and services or to help you with your personally owned computer or device.

videocam

Zoom

All University of Arizona students have free access to a Zoom account. You can join the meetings for orientation by clicking Zoom links or entering Meeting ID numbers.

What to
Expect

We are excited about “Virtual Orientation Week" and look forward to connecting with you on August 9, 2021 – August 13, 2021.

You can expect to:

  • Learn about the Advisement Report and Graduation Requirements
  • Explore the online classroom in Desire2Learn (D2L)
  • Find out what resources are available to you
  • Hear about loans, grants, scholarships, and GI Bill
  • Ask questions, and so much more!
! Each day is broken up into 30 minute sessions and will begin at 5:30 PM (Arizona time).
